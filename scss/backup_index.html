Subhash Public School

The school has 11+ years glorious history. It is one of the oldest and best school in Noneya Paharpur (East Champaran,BIHAR). It was founded on 2nd April 2011 .

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a challenging and supportive educational environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution of education that prepares our students to become responsible citizens and leaders who will make a positive impact on the world.

Facilities

Subhash Public School is the best choice for your child's education. With large, spacious classrooms, state-of-the-art equipment, and rigorous academics.

Activities

We offer a range of co-curricular and extracurricular activities that help students explore new interests, develop skills, and build a sense of community.
